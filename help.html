Help & Guide

Everything you need to know about EntranceHearts — your Nepal aspirant dating & community app

Getting Started — 4 Simple Steps
1

Create Your Account

Go to Sign Up and enter your name, email, and a strong password. Choose your exam category (CEE, IOE, +2, or Management). This helps us find your best matches!

2

Complete Your Profile

Visit Profile to add your photo, write a bio, and fill in your details. Profiles with photos get 3x more matches. Upload a clear selfie where your face is visible.

3

Discover & Match

Head to Discover to see other aspirants. Press ❤️ Like on profiles you're interested in. When someone likes you back — it's a Match! You can then chat.

4

Start Chatting

Once matched, go to Chats to send messages. You can send text, emojis, and voice messages. Long-press a message to react to it!

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get matches?
Go to Discover and press ❤️ Like on profiles you're interested in. When that person also likes you back, it becomes a mutual match and you can both start chatting. Make sure your profile has a photo and bio — it increases your chances significantly!
Why can't I see my profile photo?
After uploading your photo in Profile, it may take a few seconds to appear. Make sure you are uploading a clear JPEG or PNG image under 5MB. The photo must clearly show your face. Try refreshing the page after uploading.
Is my identity hidden on the Confession Wall?
Yes! Confessions are completely anonymous. No name, email, or account is attached. We only store a random device ID so you can delete your own posts. You can also post without creating an account.
How do I upload study materials?
You need to be logged in to upload. Go to the Study page, fill in the title, select your category (CEE/IOE/NEB/Management) and subject, write a short description, then select your file. Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, TXT, JPG, PNG. Max file size is 10MB.
Can I change my exam category later?
Yes! Go to your Profile and tap the Edit button. You can update your category, bio, interests, gender preference, and other details at any time.
Why am I not seeing any profiles in Discover?
This happens when: (1) You've already seen all available profiles — they refresh every 7 days. (2) Your gender/category filter is too narrow — try selecting "All" in the filter. (3) There are no other users in your selected category yet. Invite your friends to join!
How do I send a voice message in chat?
In the Chat screen, tap and hold the microphone button (🎤) to record a voice message. Release to send. Make sure you allow microphone permissions when the browser asks. Voice messages work on most modern phones and browsers.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the Login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link in your email inbox. Check your spam folder if it doesn't appear within a few minutes.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile, scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your profile, matches, messages, and all uploaded content. This action cannot be undone.
Can I use EntranceHearts on my phone as an app?
Yes! EntranceHearts is a PWA (Progressive Web App). Open it in your phone browser, then tap the share button and select "Add to Home Screen" (iOS) or tap the install prompt (Android). It will work just like a native app — no App Store needed!
