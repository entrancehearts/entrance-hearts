What Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name, email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile information: age, gender, exam category (CEE / IOE / +2 / Management), district, bio
  • Profile photos you upload
  • Messages you send to other users
  • Confessions you post (stored anonymously — not linked to your profile publicly)
  • Study materials you upload to the library
  • Study timer session data

Information collected automatically

  • Device type and browser information (for bug fixing)
  • Pages visited and features used within the app
  • Last active timestamp (shown as "Recently active" to matches)
  • Like / pass actions on profiles

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to operate and improve EntranceHearts:

  • Show your profile to compatible users in your exam category
  • Enable messaging between users who have mutually liked each other
  • Send push notifications for new matches and messages (only if you opt in)
  • Run the AI Match feature to suggest compatible profiles
  • Maintain the Confession Wall and Study Library
  • Track study timer sessions for your personal statistics
  • Moderate content and enforce community guidelines

🚫 We do NOT sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties. We have no advertising on this app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which is built on PostgreSQL and hosted on AWS infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using bcrypt
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
  • Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage with access controls
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Messages are stored in our database and are only accessible to the sender and recipient

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

Who We Share Data With

We share data only with essential service providers needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage provider
  • Google Fonts / Cloudflare CDN — Font and icon delivery
  • Browser APIs — Push notifications (via your device's notification system)

We may disclose information if required by Nepali law or to protect the safety of our users.

Your profile is visible to other registered users. Your confession posts are anonymous — your name is never attached to them.

Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • View your data — All your profile information is visible on your Profile page
  • Update your data — Edit your profile anytime from the Profile page
  • Delete your account — Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications — Disable notifications in your device settings or browser permissions at any time
  • Block users — Block any user from their profile to prevent all further contact
  • Report content — Report inappropriate profiles or confessions using the report button

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not tracking cookies) to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions (authentication token)
  • Remember your theme preference (dark / light mode)
  • Cache confession likes so they persist between visits
  • Remember if you dismissed the app install prompt

We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies.

Age Restrictions

EntranceHearts is intended for users who are 16 years or older, as it is designed for students preparing for entrance exams in Nepal.

If we become aware that a user under the age of 16 has provided personal information without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has created an account should contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

Continued use of EntranceHearts after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
